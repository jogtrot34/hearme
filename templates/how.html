How HearMe Works

A peer-to-peer mental health support platform connecting users in a safe, anonymous environment.

1 Sign Up & Set Up Your Profile

  • Create an account with a username (real name not required)
  • Optionally share your interests or mental health topics
  • Your privacy is protectedâ€”only necessary details are stored

2 Browse or Start Conversations

  • Find Supporters: Connect with users who have similar experiences
  • Anonymous Chat: Conversations are private unless you choose to share more
  • Topic-Based Matching: Filter chats by mental health topics

3 Chat Safely

  • Peer Support: Talk to real people who understand
  • No Pressure: Conversations can be one-time or ongoing
  • Report Concerns: Flag unsafe conversations immediately

4 Give & Receive Feedback

  • Rate conversations to help improve the platform
  • Optionally leave anonymous feedback for your chat partner

Important Disclaimers

  • Not Professional Therapy: HearMe is not a substitute for licensed therapy
  • Peer Support Only: Users are not verified professionals
  • Confidentiality: Avoid sharing personally identifiable information
  • Emergency Help: In crisis? Contact 988 Lifeline or Crisis Text Line
